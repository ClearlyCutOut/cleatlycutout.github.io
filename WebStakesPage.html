Stakes


The stakes in Balatro act as levels. There are 8 stakes in Balatro, they are, in order:
White Stake, Red Stake, Green Stake, Black Stake, Blue Stake, Purple Stake, Orange Stake,
and lastly, the Gold Stake. The stakes scale, which means the debuff in Red Stake will
be in all future stakes, and this applies to all of them. The higher the stake, the more
planning and strategizing is required.


White Stake:
The first stake available at all times. There are no debuffs and no drawbacks. Beating this
unlocks Red Stake for the deck you beat it on and when you beat your first run, you will unlock
the Blueprint Joker.


Red Stake:
The second stake. This stake makes it so that the small blind in all antes will give no money,
making getting money a little bit harder, so you can't be wasting money and be strategic with it.
Beating this stake unlocks the Green Stake for the deck you beat it on and the Zodiac Deck.


Green Stake:
The third stake. This stake increases the scaling of the blinds, making each ante
harder than they used to be in the previous stakes. Now you have to be strategic with playing
the right hands and making sure that you make enough ships to pass the blinds. Beating this
unlocks the Black Stake for the deck you beat it on and the Painted Deck.


Black Stake:
The fourth stake. This stake gives every joker a 30% chance of an Eternal Sticker. Eternal
Stickers make it so that the joker it is affecting cannot be sold or destroyed. So if you find
a joker with it, you wanna make sure that its gonna be a good joker. Beating this stake unlocks
the Blue Stake for the deck you beat it on and the Anaglyph Deck


Blue Stake:
The fifth stake. This stake takes away one of your discards. Thats really it. Makes it harder
for you to draw the cards you need. Beating this stake unlocks Purple stake for the deck you beat
it on and the Anaglyph Deck.


Purple Stake:
The sixth stake. This stake increases the scaling of the blinds again, making it even harder than
before. Even more strategy is involved in this stake. Beating this deck unlocks the Orange stake
for the deck you beat it on.


Orange Stake:
The seventh stake. This stake gives every joker a 30% chance to have a Perishable Sticker. Perishable
Stickers make the affected joker debuffed after 5 rounds, thus rendering them useless. It doesn't
make them dissappear like when the Gros Michel joker goes extinct, it just debuffs them and they
don't do anything anymore. Beating this stake unlocks the Gold Stake for the deck you beat it on
and the Erratic Deck.


Gold Stake:
The final stake in Balatro. This stake gives every joker a 30% chance to have a Rental Sticker
Rental Stickers make the affected joker $1 in the shop, but when purchased and in your joker slots,
it will take $3 from your total amount of money.

Now that you know about all of the stakes, you can climb your way to the top of the stake ladder
and you are one step closer to becoming a Balatro legend!
