About Balatro


Balatro is a poker-themed roguelike deck-building game developed by solo developer LocalThunk,
and published by Playstack. It was released for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Steam, and Xbox
on February 20, 2024, with a port to macOS on March 1, 2024. Ports for Android and iOS were released
on September 26, 2024. In Balatro, the player must score a certain number of chips by playing
poker hands from a starting 52-card deck, with a limited number of hands and discards each round.
The game was developed over a two and a half year period by solo developer LocalThunk. Initially,
LocalThunk had only treated Balatro as a side project to put on a résumé, not anticipating the success
of the game. Balatro has received universal acclaim from game critics, selling more than 5 million
copies by January 2025. Along with several other awards and nominations, Balatro was named Game of
the Year at the 25th Game Developers Choice Awards and nominated for the top prize at The Game Awards 2024,
the 28th Annual D.I.C.E. Awards, and the 21st British Academy Games Awards.


Intro to Balatro!


In Balatro, you have to score chips and multiplier by collecting good jokers and playing poker hands.
Jokers are the most important part of the game, and entire runs are based off of a good joker lineup.
All jokers have a unique ability and are all beneficial in some way to your game.
Blinds are the main obstacle that the player must overcome during a run in Balatro. For each selected Blind,
the player has to meet or surpass the required score to beat it, otherwise they will lose the run.
Stakes act as a level system, and the higher the stake, the harder the game gets.
Planet cards level up your poker hands by makeing each hand worth more chips and multiplier.
Tarot cards are the best way to make the perfect deck, they can enhance cards and cut cards from your deck.
Spectral cards in Balatro are rare, high-risk consumables that can dramatically manipulate your deck
to enable really powerful, run-defining synergies.
